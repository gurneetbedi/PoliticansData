Data collection in progress

PolitiTrack currently scrapes candidate affidavit data from ADR's myneta. Electoral-bonds and party-donation flows are a separate dataset that ADR publishes at myneta.info/electoral_bonds. A scraper for this source is on the roadmap.

Until then, the numbers shown above are illustrative based on ADR's publicly released March 2024 analysis. For citations and per-donor breakdowns, follow the source link above.
